Organ Of Harmony

This organ-shaped stage, as its name suggests, is a medium-sized stage in terms of capacity since it is located outdoors, so it currently has about 6000 people to enjoy the show without any problem.

The retro look gives it a special charm, which dazzles all festival goers. This year will have as main exponents artists of international stature such as Afrojack or Martin Garrix, as well as the unmistakable and always enjoyable Timmy Trumpet.
